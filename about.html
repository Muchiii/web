What's the Purpose?

Boston is not only a city of history, but also an academic city. With a population of 650,000 people, 250,000 of which are syudents, Boston is the perfect environment for those who are serious about learning something and want to realize their dreams through study abroad. Also, compared to big cities such as NewYork, there are fewer Japanese people here, so you can concentrate on studying Englisuh in an English-Speaking environment. You can study in a relaxed environment while enjoying the beautiful historical strees. In an environment where one out of three people living in Boston is a university student, you will be able to interact with native university students studying at some of the world's top university, as well as international students from other countries. For example, conversation among friends are often about collage and classes, so you can experience completely different ways of thinking and ideas from those in Japan. I think syudying abroad in Boston is a stimulating experience because you can feel the American university life.
