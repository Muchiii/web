About Boston

The history of Boston plays a central role in American histiry.In 1630, Puritan colonists from England founded Boston. Which quickly became the political,commercial,financial,religion and educational center of the New England region. The American Revolution erupted in Boston, as the British retaliated harshly fot the Boston Tea Party and the patriots fought back.

Tourist attractions include "Museum of Fire Arts,Boston" "Fenway Park" "The boston Publick Library" "Trinity Church" "Sky Walk Observatory". There is also the oldest ballpark in Major League Baseball and the oldest public library in the United States. Boston has a long history, and there are many historical churches and buildings to see and feel the history.
