What is the purpose of your study abroad? There are people who have always dreamed of study abroad, and people who want to actually live in the country there their favorite idol lives and use that language. Among them, many people may want to directly experience the local culture, which is a different culture. I had an experience of staying in Cebu Island in the Philipines when I was high school. It was my first visit to an Asian country. That was my first proper exposure to another culture. The experience was very valuable to me and has influenced many of my later thoughts. So, in this long-term study abroad program,I'm looking forward to seeing what kind of experience, knowledge, and personal growth I'll gain there.
